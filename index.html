ABOUT ME

Hi! I’m Candela Ribas, a graphic and UX/UI designer with a multidisciplinary, curious mind, always eager to create projects where aesthetics and experience go hand in hand. I believe great design comes from questioning everything, experimenting without fear, and playing with new ideas.

I'm versatile and curious: I move freely between visual aesthetics and UX logic. I’m always looking to give each project its own personality and make an impact. Thanks for checking out, feel free to explore!
